About Me

I am currently studying Computer Engineering at Universidad Pablo de Olavide, in Seville, Spain, and I teach programming, robotics, and technology to students from different ages. My teaching approach prioritizes logical reasoning, problem decomposition, and computational thinking before syntax, commands, or isolated tools.

To support that approach in the classroom, I have developed my own materials, from textbooks and progressive learning sequences to assessment rubrics aligned with the IB MYP, working with Scratch as a first contact with programming, Python to develop logic and algorithms in greater depth, and Arduino for robotics projects with sensors, actuators, and embedded systems. I am especially interested in international school environments where technology is taught through inquiry and real projects, not as an isolated subject.
